Catch the dogs
--------------------------
Welcome!
Theme
How many dogs can you catch...? Warning! Be careful not to fall... ;)
Instructions
W or UP ARROW keyboard - move forward
A or LEFT ARROW keyboard - move left
S or DOWN ARROW keyboard - move backward
D or RIGHT ARROW keyboard - move right
MOVES the MOUSE to change direction and to look around
POINT the MOUSE to catch the dogs
ESC to exit
-----------------------------------
Choose the level you want to play, but take in account that, more difficult is the modality you choose, more dogs you need to catch...
